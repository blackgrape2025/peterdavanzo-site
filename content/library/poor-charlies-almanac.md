# 



Charlie Munger's speeches, ideas and mental models.

He's a pleasure to read. He reduces the complexity of markets and investing to folksy observations with typical good humour. "Take your work very seriously, but never yourself."

An advocate of reading constantly, Charlie synthesised ideas from everywhere: psychology, economics, mathematics, engineering and history. He doesn't compartmentalise. Instead, he builds a lattice of mental models through which to examine problems.
