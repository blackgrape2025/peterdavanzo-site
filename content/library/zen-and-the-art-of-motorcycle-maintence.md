# 



It's not really about motorcycle maintenance. Or Zen. 

Robert Pirsig sets out to explore a simple question: what makes one thing better than another?

It's a simple question, but the answer is anything but.

Part road trip, part philosophy book, part slow-motion nervous breakdown, *Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance* shouldn't work.

Somehow it does.

Some things are recognised before they are understood.
