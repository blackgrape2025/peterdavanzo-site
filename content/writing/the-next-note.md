---
title: The Next Note
description: How do we learn to trust people we've never met? And what happens when they're not people?
date: 2026-06-11

---
I've never met Miles Davis.

But I trust his judgment more than many people I have met.

Not as a person. We never met.

He may or may not have given great financial advice. Or relationship advice.

But I trust him to choose the next note.

When I listen to his records, I can hear the emotion, the highs and lows, the atmosphere.

Earned trust.

We do this all the time.

We trust musicians we've never met.

Investors we've never met.

Writers we've never met.

We infer judgment from the work.

AI can produce answers instantly.

But we're about to go one layer up.

Just like Miles - who I have never met - I'd look for a pattern of good choices over time.

A writer. An investor. A musician. They have proof of work.

I haven't met my AI either - and never will.

I am watching its patterns.
