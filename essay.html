How I Would Build a Library for the End of the World

We are drowning in information and starving for wisdom.

If the world as we know it were to end—not with a bang, but with a long unravelling—what would be worth keeping?

Not what is most entertaining. Not what is most popular. Not what confirms us. But what is most true, most useful, most enduring.
